Privacy Policy / Gizlilik Politikasi

Last updated / Son guncelleme: March 10, 2026

1. Data Controller / Veri Sorumlusu

ConsultEdu ("we", "us", "our", "biz") is the data controller responsible for your personal data in accordance with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 ("KVKK") and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR").

Contact / Iletisim: privacy@consultedu.com

2. What Data We Collect / Toplanan Veriler

We currently operate a pre-launch landing page. The personal data we collect is limited to:

  • Email address — provided voluntarily when you sign up for our waitlist.
  • User role preference — whether you are interested as a student, counselor, or both.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies (see our Cookie Policy).

3. Purpose and Legal Basis / Amac ve Hukuki Dayanak

Under KVKK Article 5, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

Purpose / Amac Legal Basis / Hukuki Dayanak
Sending launch notifications and product updates Your explicit consent (KVKK Art. 5/1)
Analyzing interest and segmenting waitlist Legitimate interest of the data controller (KVKK Art. 5/2-f)
Ensuring website security and functionality Legitimate interest of the data controller (KVKK Art. 5/2-f)
Compliance with legal obligations Legal obligation (KVKK Art. 5/2-c)

4. Data Retention / Veri Saklama Suresi

We retain your email address and role preference for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected — specifically, until the product launches and you either create an account or unsubscribe. If you withdraw consent, your data will be deleted within 30 days.

Technical log data is retained for a maximum of 12 months.

5. Data Transfers / Veri Aktarimi

Your data may be transferred to third-party service providers who assist us in operating the waitlist and website:

  • Form processing service — to collect and store waitlist submissions.
  • Hosting provider — to serve the website.
  • Analytics provider — to understand website traffic (if enabled).

Some of these providers may be located outside of Turkey. In such cases, data transfers are conducted in compliance with KVKK Article 9, ensuring adequate protection through standard contractual clauses or the recipient country's adequate data protection status as determined by the Personal Data Protection Board (KVKK Kurulu).

6. Your Rights Under KVKK / KVKK Kapsamindaki Haklariniz

Under KVKK Article 11, you have the following rights:

  • To learn whether your personal data is being processed.
  • To request information about the processing if your data has been processed.
  • To learn the purpose of processing and whether it is used in accordance with its purpose.
  • To know the third parties to whom your personal data is transferred domestically or abroad.
  • To request correction of your data if it is incomplete or inaccurate.
  • To request deletion or destruction of your personal data under KVKK Article 7.
  • To request that corrections, deletions, or destruction be notified to third parties to whom your data has been transferred.
  • To object to any result that is against your interests arising from the analysis of your data exclusively through automated systems.
  • To claim compensation for damages arising from unlawful processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@consultedu.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days as required by law.

7. Cookies / Cerezler

We use cookies on our website. For detailed information about which cookies we use and how to manage them, please see our Cookie Policy.

8. Security Measures / Guvenlik Onlemleri

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, in accordance with KVKK Article 12. These include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission.
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular review of data processing activities.

9. Changes to This Policy / Politika Degisiklikleri

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email.

10. Contact & Complaints / Iletisim ve Sikayet

For questions or concerns about this policy or your personal data:

Email: privacy@consultedu.com

If you believe your rights have been violated, you may file a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Authority (Kisisel Verileri Koruma Kurumu - KVKK) at www.kvkk.gov.tr.

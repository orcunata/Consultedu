1. What Are Cookies / Cerezler Nedir

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit.

This Cookie Policy explains how ConsultEdu ("we", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, in compliance with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (KVKK) and the Electronic Commerce Law No. 6563.

3. Your Consent / Onayiniz

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner. You can choose to:

  • Accept all cookies — all cookie types will be enabled.
  • Accept only necessary cookies — only strictly necessary cookies will be used.

You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the website.

4. Managing Cookies in Your Browser / Tarayicinizda Cerez Yonetimi

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

Instructions for common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

5. Third-Party Cookies / Ucuncu Taraf Cerezleri

We currently use Google Fonts, which may set cookies. If we add additional third-party services (such as analytics or advertising), this policy will be updated accordingly and your consent will be requested.

6. Your Rights / Haklariniz

Under KVKK, cookies that collect personal data are subject to data protection requirements. You have the right to:

  • Know what cookies are being used and for what purpose.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Request deletion of data collected through cookies.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@consultedu.com.

7. Changes / Degisiklikler

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. If we introduce new cookie types that require consent, we will request your consent again.
